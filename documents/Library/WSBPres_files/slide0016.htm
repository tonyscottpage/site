The Client’s Relationship with US
•A deliberate (and unusual!) factor in the Bowl is that no ongoing relationship is formed with the client. So you need have no concern about disruption to your ongoing relationship with the client.
•After a session the client walks away on their own, with no further support or contact from the Bowl's coaches.
•The Bowl is a reliable process derived by Jeremy Keeley and Tony Page from best practice in the development arena. We draw from Gestalt and Tavistock traditions.
•Our deep experience of coaching, facilitating and consulting, to individual leaders and groups has been blended with practice together since 1996 of robust challenge yielding rich insights in a learning set.
