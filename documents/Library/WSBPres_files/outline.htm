What’s the deal?
  • You take away…
    • Clarity on your real issue/opportunity, a solution and a clear set of actions
    • New knowledge about yourself, your unique strengths, your way of thinking and impact as a leader
    • A way to coach others to tackle their issues and grow themselves as leaders

10
How does it work?
  • Turn up at a pre-arranged time and place, with your chosen issue in mind. They’ll be three coaches in the room. They engage you in a searching inquiry, asking you questions, challenging you to explain your issue and reflecting their direct experience of being with you


  • The process is clearly directed and enables you to discover how/why your issue is gripping you. You find keys to a different set of thoughts, feelings and action possibilities. The releasing effect typically leaves you feeling energetic, resourceful, more confident to be yourself in the world… and with plenty to think about!


  • Each session takes only half a day. The second session, in which you can elect to operate as one of the three coaches, begins with a personal briefing to frame your development objectives and ends with a review and feedback discussion with you.

13
Readiness Checklist
  • Do you have an issue or opportunity which is:
    • Real and complicated
    • Involves you significantly
    • Current and unresolved
  • Are you open to different ways of addressing the issue?
  • Are you willing to take full responsibility for your own insights and conclusions?
  • Is the possibility of resolving the issue worth it enough to justify the cost in time and fees?
  • Are you happy to travel to the IOD in Pall Mall, London?


  • And NO to this question
  • Do you know or need to work with one or more of the three coaches now or in the future? (If yes, speak to us and we’ll seek a way round).

The Client’s Relationship with US
  • A deliberate (and unusual!) factor in the Bowl is that no ongoing relationship is formed with the client. So you need have no concern about disruption to your ongoing relationship with the client.
  • After a session the client walks away on their own, with no further support or contact from the Bowl's coaches.
  • The Bowl is a reliable process derived by Jeremy Keeley and Tony Page from best practice in the development arena. We draw from Gestalt and Tavistock traditions.
  • Our deep experience of coaching, facilitating and consulting, to individual leaders and groups has been blended with practice together since 1996 of robust challenge yielding rich insights in a learning set.
