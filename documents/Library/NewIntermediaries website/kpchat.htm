The New Intermediaries ONLINE Community
The New Intermediaries ONLINE Community is a place for you to enjoy asking questions, inquiring into others' practices and experiences, within the broad frame of "people,organisations and change" and seeing what we can learn together.

Although as individuals we tend to place a high value on Face To Face contact, because we are busy people and spread out across the globe we have developed others ways of connecting. Our Yahoo group is the core: you go here to join NI and to be "in the loop" for whatever is going on.

We are also experimenting with other communication channels, specifically Teleforums (ie. telephone conference calls) and Instant Messaging (ie. real time chat using your keyboard). You'll find out more about happenings in these channels once you have subscribed to our Yahoo group. Read on to find out how.


To subscribe..

... send a blank email to newintermediaries-subscribe@yahoogroups.com. You will receive a subscription confirmation message. Simply reply this message and your subscription will be complete.

Once you have subscribed, you're "in the loop" and any messages to the group will arrive in your email, and you can reply simply by hitting the reply button. If you prefer, instead of individual emails, you can opt to view messages at the website or to receive an aggregated "daily digest" in your email.


To join in the dialogue..

...just send an email to newintermediaries@yahoogroups.com

To continue an existing dialogue topic use the same subject heading, and to start a new thread, choose your own subject heading - perhaps a question to invite, generate or provoke responses in others.


To view all the messages posted..

...go to http://groups.yahoo.com/group/newintermediaries/messages


To unsubscribe..

...just send an email to newintermediaries-unsubscribe@yahoogroups.com
