Online conversations

Whilst physically dispersed we can overcome the barriers of distance and time by making contact with others in the community online.

This can be useful when you have questions, or you want some reactions to your latest brilliant aha, or to invite others to a face to face meeting, or you just when you want to chat and connect with others.

Even when you're not wanting to join in directly the background chatter brings a reassuring sense of life "out there" and reminds you you're part of something larger.

Conversations so far have covered a wide range of topics from creativity to 360 degree feedback, Gestalt, helping survivors, large scale interventions, workshop games....

To check this out go to
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/newintermediaries/

If you would like to join in, take a deep breath and send a blank email to newintermediaries-subscribe@yahoogroups.com. You will receive a subscription confirmation message. Reply to this message and after a routine email check with a moderator your subscription will be complete.
