"Copyleft" Terms of use

This is the place where you might expect to find some relatively complicated legal text protecting authors, contributors and posters of material from legal action. At first we drafted something based on legal words used elsewhere. Read it by clicking here.

But this generated some discomfort because it conflicted with the New Intermediaries value of making our knowledge freely available within the community. One of our members, Colston Sanger, pointed us to the open source movement in software development and the idea of a "Copyleft" based on a desire to share as opposed to a "Copyright" based on a desire to restrict and protect. To read more about Copyleft from New Scientist with links and examples of "Open Cola" a fizzy drink click here.

Arising from "Copyleft", the principle here is to share, provided we can do so without violation of existing copyrights. The Terms and Conditions below are the closest we can find to guide you in providing, posting and using material in this Knowledge Pool.
