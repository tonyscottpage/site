The Journey

 

If you are ready to explore your inner and outer landscapes at the same time, why not join us on ‘The Journey’? 

Would you like to have a fascinating opportunity to:-

·        Learn more about how you function in a team?

·        Get more in touch, AND

·        Get fit.. in good company?

 

‘The Journey’ uses a stretching, facilitated walk as a metaphor for a project (or any other process).  Because it involves the whole person it enables us to explore hidden corners, behaviours and interactions with self and others that perhaps lie hidden, waiting to ‘sand-bag’ us.

If you would like to join us for this unique experience and at the same time see some of Surrey’s most stunning vistas, join us as we walk through sun-dappled woods (we hope!), climb hills and listen to tinkling streams in woodland glades.

So, put on you walking boots (cue Nancy Sinatra) and have a body, mind and spiritual workout!
