"Change from the Inside and Outside"

17 Feb 2000

Teddington

 

Purpose

This was an initial meeting for people working to bring about change from inside their organisation interested in entering dialogue with each other and with people who work from the outside in. Lots of these internal/external collaborations take place everyday but how often do they produce real synergy and learning? This meeting set out to share perspectives and explore the issues and opportunities at the internal/external boundary.
