The New Intermediaries ONLINE Community
The New Intermediaries ONLINE Community is a place for you to enjoy asking questions, inquiring into others' practices and experiences, within the broad frame of "people,organisations and change" and seeing what we can learn together, and no doubt learning more about how we learn.

This shows you how to find out more, and how to join in.


To see who is signed up already..

...go to http://www.egroups.com/members/newintermediaries


To subscribe..

...go to http://www.egroups.com/subscribe/newintermediaries

... or send a blank email to newintermediaries-subscribe@eGroups.com. You will receive a subscription confirmation message. Simply reply this message and your subscription will be complete.


To join in the dialogue..

...just send an email to newintermediaries@egroups.com

To continue an existing dialogue topic use the same subject heading, and to start a new thread, choose your own subject heading - perhaps a question to invite, generate or provoke responses in others.


To view all the messgages posted..

...go to http://www.egroups.com/messages/newintermediaries


To unsubscribe..

...just send an email to newintermediaries-unsubscribe@egroups.com


Any problems..

...please contact one of your moderators: Colston Sanger colston@shotters.dircon.co.uk or Tony Page tonypage@dircon.co.uk
