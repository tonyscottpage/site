Change sponsor research

Andy Harmon and Tony Page have embarked on a research project which seeks to understand from sponsors of change the role of improvisation and authentic communication.

This year they have interviewed ten change sponsors from a range of public and private sector organisations including Guinness, Prison Service, McKinsey, NAAFI, Royal Opera House, British Aerospace, Royal Armouries, Price Waterhouse Coopers and NPI.

Each interview lasting around 90 minutes draws from the change sponsor their own story of a change. A full transcript is produced and analysed.

Andy's background in improvisation theatre and Tony's in organisational change is helping to produce new insights in the emerging conversations with change sponsors. They are developing a dramatic metaphor that casts light on the sponsor's role in working through CONFLICT to achieve organisational transformation.

Their article currently in draft form is due to be published later.
