New Intermediaries - an artisan community

We follow a long tradition in which artisans from builders to artists meet informally in pubs and coffee houses. We are an open community - a community of practice - not an organisation. People come and go, dip in and out as work and energy levels permit.

Our field of practice is "people, change and organisation". Our activities are non-commercial, in pursuit of fun, learning, making connections. From our activities we generate know-how which we use in our organisational consulting work.

Our distinctive character and activities are developing along the following lines:

OPEN, not membership based
deeply EGALITARIAN not an elite
TWO-WAY RECIPROCAL not one way interaction
activities are MANY SIDED & VARIED, not one sided, one activity
an EXPLORATORY JOURNEY, not promising any specific outcome
events are for EVERYONE to relate to, not narrow or theoretical
a spirit of GENEROUS SHARING, not proprietorial - this is mine
events are for JOINING IN, not for BEING talked or lectured at
DEVELOPING KNOW-HOW TOGETHER, not selling to each other
we are a LIVE EXPERIMENT in new ways of working.
