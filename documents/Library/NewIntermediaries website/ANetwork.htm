Who are we?
New Intermediaries is a group of people who share a common interest in introducing change to people and organisations.

Why 'new'?
Because many of us have found that previous change efforts have left a legacy of confusion, fatigue and defensiveness. Leaders and ocnsultants need to find new ways of engaging people and creating momentum if they are to succeed in our emerging, new, global economy.

Why 'intermediaries'?
Because the answer may lie in the kind s of relationships we build; redefining our role as bridge-builders or catalysts who draw the right people together into forthright conversations.

Why a network?
Initially to provide opportunities for contact, experimentation and support. To share learning and get energy from working and thinking together. And in time to define our place in the world as we recognise what we can achieve together.
