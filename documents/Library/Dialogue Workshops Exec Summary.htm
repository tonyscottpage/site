Dialogue Workshops for Intact Teams

 

Executive Summary

_________________________________

 

The promise of Dialogue is to make our conversations less frustrating and more productive. In a Dialogue Workshop participants are encouraged to pay special attention to the impact of how they are talking to one another, and to experiment with different skills to create a meeting of minds.

 

Such skills are relevant to all conversations where communication, decision-making, conflict resolution, problem-solving and creativity are important. Dialogue tends to teach people how to be more authentic, open, direct and constructive with each other. People learning these skills become more aware and have more choices rather than being locked into ingrained habits.

 

If you delve further in this section you can find some stories, some practices and principles arising from recent experiences with colleagues of running Dialogue Workshops with intact executive teams, that you might find useful in deciding whether how to go down this path with your team.

 

The approach in any particular Dialogue Workshop is not pre-determined and arises in discussion with the client to suit the needs and situation of those involved.

 

 

Enjoy!
